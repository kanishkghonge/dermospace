Dr. Venkatesh Mandava is a highly skilled Dermatologist, Aesthetic Dermatologist, and Cosmetologist practicing at Dermospace in MVP Colony, Visakhapatnam. With over 12 years of clinical experience, he is dedicated to delivering evidence-based, patient-first skin and hair care.

He completed his MD in Dermatology, Venereology & Leprosy from the prestigious Kalinga Institute of Medical Sciences, Bhubaneswar in 2020, and his MBBS from JJMMC Davangere, RGUHS Bangalore in 2014.

Known for his patience, attention to detail, and customised treatment approach, Dr. Venkatesh listens carefully to each patient's concerns and tailors his recommendations accordingly — never rushing, always thorough.
